## What is Surf?

Surf is a crypto-native AI platform that goes beyond Q&A — it analyzes, reports, builds, and deploys. Think of it as an AI that actually understands crypto: real-time on-chain data, market sentiment, and agentic execution in one prompt.

**Backed by** Pantera Capital, Coinbase Ventures, and DCG.

## Build Ideas

Looking for inspiration? Here are some things the community is building:

- **Arbitrage Monitor** — Track cross-exchange funding rates and spot-perp spreads in real-time
- **Listing Tracker** — Monitor new token listings and analyze 30-day post-listing performance
- **Sentiment Dashboard** — Twitter sentiment vs. on-chain data divergence alerts
- **Portfolio Watcher** — Multi-wallet asset monitoring with AI-powered risk signals
- **Alpha Signal Engine** — Aggregate KOL calls with on-chain smart money validation
